Welcome to CSSS 508: Introduction to R for Social Scientists

University of Washington, Spring 2017

Instructor: Charles Lanfear, PhD student, UW Sociology

Canvas page for course (enrolled students only)

Syllabus

Helpful resources:
  • R for Data Science online textbook by Garrett Grolemund and Hadley Wickham. One of many good R texts available, but importantly it is free and focuses on the tidyverse collection of R packages which form the backbone of this course.
  • Advanced R online textbook by Hadley Wickham. A great source for more in-depth and advanced R programming.
  • Jenny Bryan's materials for STAT545 at UBC. CSSS508 is based on Rebecca Ferrell's abbreviated version of Bryan's course, so more explanations can be found on her page.
  • Instructions for using RStudio in the CSSCR lab (hopefully week 1 only; I would prefer you become comfortable using your own computers!)
  • Homework Instructions and grading rubric.
  • Peer Review Instructions and suggestions for reading code.
